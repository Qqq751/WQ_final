Project Rationale

Why interactive 3D?

Instead of confining the 3D element to a small box, this iteration integrates Three.js directly into the DOM architecture. The holographic glass material responds to the user's scroll position and cursor coordinates, making the entire website feel alive and deeply interconnected.

Interaction Stack

  • 01. CURSOR PARALLAX Lights and geometry subtly track the mouse vector.
  • 02. SCROLL-DRIVEN TIMELINE Window scroll events map directly to camera Y-axis and object rotation.
  • 03. ACTIVE DEFORMATION Mousedown events trigger a lerp (linear interpolation) that scales the object and intensifies refraction.
